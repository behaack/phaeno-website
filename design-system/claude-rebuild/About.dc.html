About Us

Mapping the functional human genome, one full-length isoform at a time

Phaeno's mission is to give scientists the technology to map the functional human genome and understand what actually drives phenotype.

Our founders noted fundamental conflicts with the Central Dogma of Molecular Biology as early as 1988 — conflicts that became harder to ignore in the decades of molecular neurobiology research that followed. Testing them properly meant building new technology: something that could profile every RNA arising from every gene in a cell or tissue, simultaneously, comprehensively, accurately, and economically.

That technology is PSeq — capable of detecting the thousands of quanta of genetic information created in messenger and functional RNAs by complex steps in RNA processing that gene-level sequencing simply averages away.
